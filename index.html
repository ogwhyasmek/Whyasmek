Born in the

An urban city kid from Los Angeles who discovered a passion for graffiti artistry. Transitioning from street art, honing digital skills through Visual Media Arts and Marketing studies at Emerson College.

The journey evolved from creating online brand identities in the early 20s to fostering a digital space fueled by pure passion. This site channels everything from street photography to gaming and beyond.
